Frequently Asked Questions

What materials are used in tribal dance costumes?
Feathers, beads, shells, and hand-woven fabrics form the essence of tribal attire.
What’s the meaning behind tribal dance?
They symbolize harmony with nature, harvest celebration, spiritual rituals, and tribal unity.
Which dance uses balancing acts?
Hojagiri, from Tripura, is known for balancing acts on bottles and plates.
What instruments are used?
Drums (dhol, nagara), flutes, bamboo rattles, and rhythmic claps are common.
